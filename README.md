# -Cryptalend-Crypto-Backed-Lending-Platform

## Overview

Cryptalend is a custodial crypto-backed lending platform providing loans to retail and institutional borrowers using Bitcoin and Ethereum as collateral. The platform enables users to unlock liquidity without selling their digital assets while maintaining long-term exposure to crypto markets.

Cryptalend focuses on conservative risk management, fixed interest rates, and transparent custody reporting.

---

## What Cryptalend Does

Cryptalend allows borrowers to deposit Bitcoin or Ethereum as collateral and receive loans through cryptocurrency transfers or traditional bank transfers. This model helps investors access capital without triggering taxable asset sales.

The platform supports both individual investors and institutional clients seeking structured crypto-backed financing.

---

## Custodial Model and KYC

Cryptalend operates as a custodial platform. All collateral is securely held under platform custody controls. Borrowers must complete Know Your Customer verification to ensure regulatory compliance and platform security.

KYC requirements help prevent fraud and enable global financial operations across jurisdictions.

---

## Loan Products

Cryptalend offers two primary loan structures.

Interest-based loans allow borrowers to pay interest periodically and repay principal at the end of the term.

Amortized loans allow borrowers to repay both principal and interest in scheduled installments.

These options support both retail liquidity needs and institutional financing structures.

---

## Loan Disbursement and Processing Time

Borrowers can receive loan funds through crypto transfers or bank transfers.

Crypto disbursements are typically processed within 2 to 5 hours after approval. Bank transfers may take 24 to 48 hours depending on jurisdiction and banking infrastructure.

This hybrid payout system connects on-chain liquidity with traditional finance.

---

## Loan-to-Value Policy and Liquidation

Cryptalend offers loans within a 10-65% LTV (Loan To Value) to  reduce liquidation risk and help borrowers manage volatility.

Liquidation monitoring begins when LTV approaches 80%. At this point the borrower is  notified  to add collateral or reduce outstanding debt before liquidation thresholds are reached.

---

## Interest Rates and Credit Requirements

Our interest  rates at 10%. No credit checks are required. Eligibility is based solely on crypto collateral.

This structure is to make sure we provides predictable borrowing costs and global access to capital.

---

## No Rehypothecation Policy

We follows a strict no rehypothecation policy.  This means that Borrower's  collateral is not reused, lent, or leveraged by the platform.

This policy helps us to ensures asset segregation and reduces counterparty risk.

---

## Transparency and Proof of Custody

We publish the total amount of collateral held in custody every quarter. This report will be made  four times per year to enhance accountability for retail and institutional users.

---

## Customer Support

Our customer service is active 24/7 for loan applications, collateral management, and account assistance. This is to encourage  fast response times and borrower education.
